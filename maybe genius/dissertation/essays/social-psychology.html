Social Psychology

Understanding how individuals think, feel, and behave in social contexts

Current Research

  • Group Dynamics

    Examining how individuals behave in groups and the influence of social norms.

  • Social Cognition

    Investigating how people perceive, think about, and remember social information.

  • Prejudice and Discrimination

    Understanding the psychological roots of bias and strategies for reducing prejudice.

  • Social Influence

    Studying how others affect our thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.

Conclusion

Social psychology provides important insights into human behavior in social contexts. Understanding these principles can help improve interpersonal relationships, group dynamics, and societal interactions. Continued research in this field promises to enhance our understanding of social behavior and its applications.

Written by Hope Gilbert
Durham University
