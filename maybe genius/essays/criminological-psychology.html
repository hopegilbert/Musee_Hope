Criminological Psychology

Understanding the psychological factors behind criminal behavior and the criminal justice system

Current Research

  • Psychological Profiling and Criminal Behavior

    Examining the psychological characteristics and patterns that contribute to criminal behavior.

  • Rehabilitation and Recidivism

    Investigating psychological approaches to offender rehabilitation and reducing reoffending.

  • Eyewitness Testimony and Memory

    Analyzing the reliability of eyewitness accounts and memory in criminal investigations.

  • Forensic Psychology in Court

    Exploring the role of psychological expertise in legal proceedings and decision-making.
