Face Recognition

Exploring the cognitive and neural mechanisms behind facial perception and recognition

Current Research

  • Neural Basis of Face Processing

    Investigating the brain regions and networks involved in facial recognition and perception.

  • Developmental Aspects of Face Recognition

    Studying how face recognition abilities develop from infancy through adulthood.

  • Face Recognition Disorders

    Understanding prosopagnosia and other face recognition impairments.

  • Cross-Cultural Face Perception

    Examining how cultural factors influence face recognition and interpretation.
